Layout

Our logo consists of an icon (the circle K's) and the Kelly Klee wordmark. You can use the icon as a shorter version of our logo, but only in cases where we've already established Kelly Klee's brand. While the icon can appear without the wordmark, the wordmark should never appear without the icon.

Wordmark

Combined: We have two versions of our combined logo a vertical arrangement where the icon appears above the wordmark and a horizontal layout where the icon and wordmark are side-by-side.


Stacked

Side-by-side

Design Note: In addition to showing the wordmark and icon appear together as a combined logo, you can separate the two in your design. For example, on our website, the wordmark is left justified while the icon is centered.

Color Use

There are several color options to allow for flexibility in designs. Here are guidelines to help you choose what color to use in different situations.

Gold: Only on backgrounds that are either white or one of our brand background colors
Blue: Light backgrounds including white and our brand colors
White: Dark backgrounds
Black: Only when color isn't an option

With photos

Use the monochromatic versions — either white if the background is dark or Oxford blue if the background is light.

Size

Spacing: Don't forget to let it breathe. A good rule of thumb is to allow 1/5 of X (the size from top to bottom) spacing on all sides of the logo.

Minium size: The logo must appear clear and legible, and large enough to make an impression.

Icon
  • Print: 1.25” wide
  • Web: 30px wide.
 
Wordmark
  • Print: 3.5” wide
  • Web: 160px wide.
